Craigslist UX Redesign Mock-up

This was the final project for CGT 25600, which was named, "Principles Of User Experience Design."

All of the students in the class formed into groups and were tasked with redesinging Craigslist, a website that is famous for its 1990's design and lack of features.

My team and I made sure to put lots of effort into establishing an effective mood board for our mock-up and used the principles from our class to make meaningful changes to the layout and features.

Specifically, we wanted to improve the user experience when making a post and using the search features on craigslist. Our representative user was a graduating senior at Purdue that needed to sublease their apartment via Craigslist.

This project was worked on for around a month and was finalized into a report that can be found here.
